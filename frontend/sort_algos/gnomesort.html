Gnome sort, nicknamed stupid sort, is a variation of the insertion sort sorting algorithm that does not use nested loops

Gnome Sort is based on the technique used by the standard Dutch Garden Gnome, works by building a sorted list one element at a time, getting each item to the proper place in a series of swaps
